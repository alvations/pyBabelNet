Open Multilingual Wordnet

Documentation of and links to data for wordnets in 23 languages
 (Albanian, Arabic, Danish, English, Persian, Finnish, French, Hebrew, Italian, Japanese, Basque, Catalan, Galician, Spanish,
  Indonesian, Malaysian, Norwegian (Bokmal and Nynorsk), Portuguese, Thai, Greek, Polish, Chinese, Swedish). Size, quality and license varies widely, 
  but all licenses are open licenses that allow redistribution. 

info: http://www.casta-net.jp/~kuribayashi/multi/
